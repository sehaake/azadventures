Outdoor Activities

Markets, farms, horseback rides, and outdoor parks that give you time outside without needing a full hike or camping trip.

Puzzle Ride

Scottsdale, AZ

A puzzle-based adventure around Old Town Scottsdale where you ride in an open vehicle and solve clues along a themed route. It feels like a mix between a scavenger hunt and a guided tour.

Location: Scottsdale, AZ
Puzzle Rides - Scottsdale

The Valley Girl Market

Scottsdale, AZ

A pop-up style outdoor market in Old Town Scottsdale featuring local vendors, handmade goods, and a very Instagram-friendly setup. Good for browsing, shopping, and discovering small businesses.

Location: Old Town Scottsdale, AZ
Instagram @thevalleygirlmarket

KOLI Equestrian Horseback Rides

Chandler, AZ ~45 min away

Scenic horseback rides through the desert in south Phoenix, with options like sunset rides, group rides, and longer experiences that include games and food. It is a calm but memorable way to experience the desert landscape.

Address: 6940 W Broken Ear Rd, Chandler, AZ 85226
Koli Equestrian

Honeybrook Farm Store

Gilbert, AZ ~45 min away

A small farm space in Gilbert where you can visit and see animals like zebras and camels. It has a relaxed, family farm feel and is a fun spot for animal lovers and content photos.

Address: 3545 S Greenfield Rd, Gilbert, AZ 85297
Instagram @honeybrookfarmstore @platesandparadise's TikTok

Pecan Lake Entertainment

Queen Creek, AZ ~1 hour away

An outdoor entertainment area with electric go-karts, a ropes course, escape rooms, a putting course, and an arcade. It is a good one-stop option if you want a mix of activities in the same place.

Address: 25004 S 206th St, Queen Creek, AZ 85142
Go Pecan Lake

Out of Africa Wildlife Park

Camp Verde, AZ ~1 hr 45 min away

A wildlife park and zoo with around 400 animals, including lions, bears, leopards, and more. Shows and safaris make it feel more interactive than a traditional zoo, and it is a full-day outing.

Address: 3505 AZ-260, Camp Verde, AZ 86322
Out of Africa Park
