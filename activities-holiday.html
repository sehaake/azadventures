Holiday Activities

Seasonal events and holiday experiences around the Valley and beyond, from fall festivals and pumpkin patches to Christmas lights and winter celebrations.

Pumpkin Fest at the Princess

Scottsdale, AZ

A fall festival at the Fairmont Scottsdale Princess with pumpkin displays, rides, maze-style attractions, themed treats, and cozy seasonal decor. It is a good option if you want a more polished, resort-style Halloween and autumn experience.

Address:Fairmont Scottsdale Princess, 7575 E Princess Dr, Scottsdale, AZ 85255
Pumpkin Fest as the Princess

Harvest Nights at DH Farms

Phoenix, AZ ~1 hour away

A seasonal fall event with lights, decor, and farm-style activities that make it feel like a cozy autumn evening out. It is a good option if you want something festive without driving too far from the city.

Address:2209 N 99th Ave, Phoenix, AZ 85037

Schnepf Farms Pumpkin Patch

Queen Creek, AZ ~1 hour away

A family farm with a pumpkin patch, rides, games, and classic fall activities. This is a good choice if you want a more traditional farm-style autumn day with plenty of photo spots, food, and seasonal entertainment.

Address:24810 S Rittenhouse Rd, Queen Creek, AZ 85142
Schnepf Farms Pumpkin Patch

Christmas at the Princess

Scottsdale, AZ

A large holiday event with millions of lights, ice skating, themed photo spots, characters, and rides at the Fairmont Scottsdale Princess. It feels like a full winter festival and is especially good for people who want a very immersive Christmas experience.

Address: Fairmont Scottsdale Princess, 7575 E Princess Dr, Scottsdale, AZ 85255
Christmas at the Princess

Phoenix ZooLights

Phoenix, AZ ~30 min away

A classic Phoenix holiday tradition with light displays throughout the zoo, glowing animal figures, music, and seasonal snacks. It is a good pick for walking around with friends or family and taking pictures with colorful lights and themed scenes.

Address:Papago Park, 455 N Galvin Pkwy, Phoenix, AZ 85008
Phoenix Zoolights

Pratt Brothers Christmas

Chandler, AZ ~45 min away

A Christmas experience known for its synchronized light displays, snowfall, music, and detailed decorations. It is a great choice if you like over-the-top holiday lights and want a destination that feels like a full show rather than just a simple drive-through display.

Address:5700 W North Loop Rd, Chandler, AZ 85226
