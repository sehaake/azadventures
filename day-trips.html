Day Trips

Fun, easy day trips within driving distance of Scottsdale curated for unique, scenic Arizona adventures. *Locations are approximate and based on the Scottsdale area.*

Tortilla Flat

Tonto Basin, AZ ~1 hr away

A tiny old-west style stop with a saloon, wooden storefronts, prickly pear ice cream, and one of the prettiest drives in the state. It's quirky, a little random, and super fun day trip.

The Dolly Steamboat

Apache Junction, AZ ~1 hr away

A peaceful 1.5–3 hour steamboat tour of Canyon Lake with desert views, wildlife, and photo-perfect lake scenery.

Strawberry, Arizona

~1 hr 45 min away

A pine-tree escape with cozy mountain-town vibes. Strawberry is a gateway to Fossil Creek, an adventurous water hike with waterfalls and turquoise pools.

Pine Creek Lavender Farm Store & Cooking School

Pine, AZ ~1 hr 45 min away

A quiet and pretty lavender farm with a small shop and seasonal experiences. The entire place smells amazing, and the lavender fields make it feel like a tiny countryside escape.

Jerome, Arizona

~2 hrs away

A historic mining town built into the mountainside with scenic lookouts and unique art shops. Its real appeal is the haunted history. Many buildings are believed to be active with paranormal activity, and guided ghost tours take visitors through old hotels, mines, and eerie streets that give the town its unforgettable atmosphere.

Verde Canyon Railroad

Clarkdale, AZ ~2 hrs away

A four-hour narrated train ride through a beautiful canyon with stunning views, photo opportunities, open-air cars, and tons to learn along the way. Very relaxing and surprisingly scenic.
