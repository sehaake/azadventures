Grand Canyon Area

Different ways to turn the Grand Canyon into an overnight experience, from classic rim views to bigger trips like Havasu Falls and add-ons like Grand Canyon West Zipline.

Grand Canyon South Rim

The most popular way to see the canyon, with overlooks, visitor centers, and rim trails. Staying overnight nearby lets you catch sunrise or sunset and explore at a slower pace instead of rushing.

Havasu Falls

A more intense overnight trip that starts with a ~4 hour drive to Hualapai Hilltop and then a 10-mile hike to the falls. It’s a permit-only, backpacking-style trip, but the turquoise water and canyon walls make it one of the most famous hikes in Arizona.

Grand Canyon West Zipline

Located at Hualapai Point, this zipline lets you ride over the desert canyon landscape near Grand Canyon West. It’s a good option if you want something more active and adrenaline-focused to pair with your Grand Canyon trip.
