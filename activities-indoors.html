Indoor Activities

Indoor slides, art experiences, museums, and creative studios that are perfect for days when you want to stay inside but still do something fun and different.

Slick City Action Park

Scottsdale, AZ

An indoor slide park with high-speed slides, attractions, and a playful atmosphere. It is great for groups of friends who want something energetic that feels different than a typical arcade or movie night.

Address: The Pavilions at Talking Stick Shopping Center, 9189 E Talking Stick Wy, Scottsdale, AZ 85250
Slick City - Scottsdale

Museum of Illusions Scottsdale

Scottsdale, AZ

A hands-on museum filled with optical illusions, perspective tricks, and interactive rooms. It works well for photos, short visits, and anyone who likes playful, mind-bending spaces.

Address: Arizona Boardwalk, 9500 East V√≠a de Ventura, Scottsdale, AZ 85256
Museum of Illusions Scottsdale

Bam Kazam Escape Rooms

Scottsdale, AZ

An escape-room style attraction with multiple themed rooms and challenges. It is more active and theatrical than a traditional escape room, with sets that feel like you are stepping into a game.

Address: The Pavilions at Talking Stick Shopping Center, 8999 E Talking Stick Wy, Scottsdale, AZ 85250
Bam Kazam - Scottsdale

Puttshack

Scottsdale, AZ

A two-story, tech-infused mini-golf venue with neon visuals, music, and a full restaurant and bar. It is a good pick for night-out vibes with something interactive to do while you hang out.

Address: Scottsdale Quarter, 15059 N Scottsdale Rd, Scottsdale, AZ 85254
Puttshack - Scottsdale

Candle Chemistry

Scottsdale, AZ

A custom candle-building studio where you choose scents, pour your own candle, and then return to pick it up after it cools. It is a slower-paced, cozy activity that works well for small groups or date nights.

Address: Scottsdale Quarter, 15323 North Scottsdale Road, Suite 140, Scottsdale, AZ 85254
Candle Chemistry - Scottsdale

Jade & Clover

Phoenix, AZ ~30 min away

A bright, plant-focused shop where you can build custom planters and terrariums. It has a creative, artsy atmosphere that is perfect for people who love plants, decor, and hands-on projects.

Address: 100 W Portland St #105, Phoenix, AZ 85003
Jade & Clover

Spin Art Nation

Phoenix, AZ ~30 min away

A studio for spin art, splatter painting, fluid art, and even a rage room. It is a fun pick if you want to get messy on purpose and leave with a one of a kind art piece.

Address: 2302 N Central Ave #3, Phoenix, AZ 85004
Spin Art Nation - Phoenix

Dorrance Dome at Arizona Science Center

Phoenix, AZ ~30 min away

An immersive 8K planetarium experience that blends science, art, and culture. Shows can range from classic night sky tours to creative, music-driven visuals, making it a unique indoor option.

Address: Arizona Science Center, 600 E Washington St, Phoenix, AZ 85004
Dorrance Dome - Arizona Science Center @southwestsampler's TikTok

Phoenix Art Museum

Phoenix, AZ ~30 min away

A large art museum with rotating exhibitions, permanent collections, and occasional special events. It is a good choice for a calmer day focused on wandering through galleries and getting inspired.

Address: 1625 N Central Ave, Phoenix, AZ 85004
Phoenix Art Museum

Andretti Indoor Karting & Games

Chandler, AZ ~45 min away

A large indoor entertainment center with high-speed go-karts, arcade games, a ropes course, and more. It works well for groups that want an all-in-one spot to spend several hours.

Address: Chandler Airport Center, 1712 S Cooper Rd, Chandler, AZ 85286
Andretti Karting - Chandler
